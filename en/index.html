Flat (apartment) in Les Houches (Chamonix) for 2 / 3 persons


 

Presentation video

Descrption:

Small flat of 18 m2 for 2/3 persons in the town of Les Houches, 5 minutes from Chamonix and 45 minutes from Geneva.

The flat has a main room with kitchenette and a bathroom with shower, sink and toilet. Private parking outside and cellar in the ground floor. Quiet and secured place.

It is located close to the cable car and the ski slopes (Telecabine Prarion in Les Houches - Saint-Gervais) near the "Lac de Chavants" (trout fishing) with climbing wall and tennis courts within 100 m. Shops, bars and restaurants also available  nearby.

The apartment is very economic: Annual property tax 2013 (taxe fonciere) of 303 Euros
